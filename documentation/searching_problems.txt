*The 8-puzzle:
	Is a 3x3 board with eight numbered tiles and a blank space.
	Goal state:
	1 2 3
	8   4
	7 6 5
	Operators:blank moves left, right, up or down.
	States: a state description specifies the location of each of the eight tiles 	in one of the nine squares, also include the position of the blank.
	Problem: Is defined for an initial position of the eight tiles.
